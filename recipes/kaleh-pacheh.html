Kaleh Pacheh

It would be delicious if you can ignore what are you eating!!

If you accept eating brain eye tounge and other part of lamb head follow the recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lamb's head
  • 4 lamb leg

Steps

  1. Put the head and legs in the pot and foll it with water
  2. Heat the pot for 12 hours
  3. Your kalapch is ready
